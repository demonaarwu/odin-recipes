Traditional Chinese Egg Fried Rice

Description

Egg fried rice is a basic dish in Chinese dinning table. It's quick, easy to make, but it tastes very well. Try to make it, and you will like it!

Ingredients

  • One cup water
  • Two tablespoons soy sauce
  • One half teaspoon salt
  • One cup uncooked instant Rice
  • One teaspoon vegetable oil
  • One half onion, finely chopped
  • One half cup green beans
  • One large egg, lightly beaten
  • One forth teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Bring water, soy sauce, and salt to a boil in a medium saucepan. Stir in rice and remove from heat. Cover and let stand for five minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a medium skillet or wok over medium heat. Saute onions and green beans in hot oil for two to three minutes. Pour in beaten egg and fry for two minutes, scrambling egg while it cooks.
  3. Add cooked rice to egg mixture; mix well. Season with pepper.
